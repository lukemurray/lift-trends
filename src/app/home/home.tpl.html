Lift Trends View on GitHub

Find relationships between your Lift.do habits

Upload your Habits export from Lift.do below

   
  1. Log into your Lift.do account here
  2. Click the cog at the top right
  3. Click "Settings"
  4. Scroll down to "Export"
  5. Click the "Export" button
  6. Come back here to upload your CSV
Nothing is kept on the server. Click why above to find out more.

Simply, I wanted to see if there were any correlations between my 'habits'. This is because my partner and I use Lift to track a range of things like certain pain or what types of food we ate.

Why Upload?

Currently at 24 July 2013, Lift.do does not provide an API of any kind. Or at least not that I could find. They do however provide the ability to export your habits and check-ins.

I do not store or keep any of your habit information. After the file is uploaded it is transferred back to your browser in another format and removed from the server. See the server.js code for more information.
